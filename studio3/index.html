Froggy Fight of Fortune

  • There are 2 players.
  • Player who has the turn rolls the dice.
  • Total of roll is added to current player's score unless player rolls a "one," lilypad with holes.
  • Each time player earns points, they have a choice to roll again or pass their turn.
  • If player rolls two "ones," current score turns to zero, aka "snake eyes."
  • First player to get 30 lilypads or higher WINS!
